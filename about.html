About us..

Welcome to RajaRata Motors, your ultimate destination for high-quality motorcycle spare parts and accessories. At RajaRata Motors, we are passionate about motorcycles and understand the unique bond between riders and their machines. We're here to provide you with the best parts and services to keep your ride running smoothly and looking its best.

Our Story

Founded in 2004, RajaRata Motors has been a trusted name in the motorcycle community for over a decade. It all started with a small shop and a big dream: to become the go-to place for motorcycle enthusiasts seeking top-notch spare parts, unmatched expertise, and exceptional customer service. Over the years, we've grown, learned, and adapted to the ever-changing motorcycle industry. Today, we stand proud as a premier one-stop shop for motorcycle spare parts, catering to riders of all makes and models.

Our Commitment

At RajaRata Motors, our commitment to our customers is unwavering. We understand that your motorcycle is more than just a machine; it's an extension of your passion and personality. That's why we go the extra mile to ensure that you find the right parts and accessories to match your needs and style. We stock a wide range of genuine OEM parts and aftermarket accessories, all carefully selected to meet the highest quality standards. Our team of experts is always on hand to provide advice, answer your questions, and assist you in finding the perfect parts for your bike.
